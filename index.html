Guildrules

  • Humor should not personally offend anyone, so be mindful of others
  • Raids will be announced ingame and via Discord
  • Respect each other and uphold old-school values
  • Be fair to your fellow players, share your experience, and offer a helping hand if possible
  • Deep political debates are not welcome in the public guild area
  • Disagreements should be addressed and discussed, so seek conversation
  • Have fun and don’t take yourself too seriously

    • Clan-Image

  • We are good-natured, but not naive
  • We respect anyone who does not stand against us
  • Outwardly we may appear dark, but at our core we are well-meaning members of the Horde
  • We are always loyal to the guild and treat everyone within it equally
  • We have humor, but we do not use it to personally hurt anyone
  • Forgiveness is given, but nothing is forgotten. Those who do not learn will feel the wrath of our soul.

    • Gildenbeitritt

  • You are 18+
  • PvE/PvP – both are welcome
  • Casual players as well as dedicated WoW grinders are warmly welcome
  • No prior experience is required
  • Joining Discord is not mandatory, but topics are discussed there in more detail
  • Contact me ingame via /w Devilpope, here on the homepage, or on Discord for inquiries or membership requests

    Guildmaster

    Devilpope

    I am 28 years young, have been gaming for more than 17 years, and keep coming back to WoW. I have the vision of founding a guild where you can feel at home — a place of joy and fun, but also where bonds between players are formed. This should be a place to relax. I am a very laid-back yet organized person, and I try to take care of everyone’s problems.

    The Holys

    <

    In our guild, we distinguish between Holy and Evil — which simply refers to PvE and PvP. Those who choose PvE within the guild receive the title “Holy.” This way, it becomes clear which part of WoW each member is more specialized in. This creates structure and forms the core of the guild. Nevertheless, everyone is equal and has the same rights, with one exception: only the Holies may participate in non-PvE events. Raids, however, are open to both groups.

    The Evils

    In our guild, we distinguish between Holy and Evil — which simply refers to PvE and PvP. Those who choose PvP within the guild receive the title “Devil.” This makes it clear which part of WoW each member is more specialized in. This creates structure and forms the core of the guild. Nevertheless, everyone is equal and has the same rights, with one exception: only the Evil may participate in PvP events. Battlegrounds, however, are open to both groups.
